~ ~ ~ ~ ~ Why Should You Vote? ~ ~ ~ ~ ~

The 2020 election "may be in the hands of youth voters," says Marilyn La Jeunesse from Teen Vogue. 27% of all eligble voters are youth voters, which means that they have the ability to decide 27% of all votes. When put into perspective, youth voters have the power to determine whether a candidate wins the election or not. So why haven't they? The turnout of the 2016 election showed that only half of voters between the ages 18 and 25 cast their ballot. Youth voters vote even less in midterm elections, amounting from 20-25% of the total votes. Voting is a right, and all voters, including young voters, should excercise this right to decide the future leader of our nation.
